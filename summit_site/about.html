About Summit Farms Rescue

Summit Farms is recognized for rescuing Alaskan dog breeds such as Malamutes and Huskies. Our mission is to adopt and save as many dogs as we can.

We believe in helping the helpless and reducing the number of homeless dogs while improving the welfare of all domestic animals. Additionally, we seek to educate our community on the plight of these animals.

Brad and Janet Harker founded Summit Farms Rescue in 2004. We work to foster Respect, Understanding and Compassion for all creatures, and the belief that all life is valuable and worthy of protection.

Since the founding of Summit Farms, we have adopted more than 2,620 dogs and counting.

We are grateful for the continued support of our donors. We are overjoyed to have so many huskies have new forever homes with caring families. Should you have any questions, please feel free to visit our Contact Us page.

Summit Farms normally has the following breeds:

  • Alaskan Husky
  • Alaskan Malamute
  • Alaskan Klee Kai
  • American Eskimo
  • Siberian Husky
  • Samoyed
  • Greenland Seppala Siberian Sled Dog

About Alaskan Dog Breeds

Alaskan Malamute

The Alaskan Malamute is one of the oldest Arctic sled dogs. Known for strength and endurance, they were essential to early explorers.

Siberian Husky

Siberian Huskies are medium-sized working dogs, affectionate and energetic, originally bred by the Chukchi people of Siberia.

Alaskan Husky

The Alaskan Husky is not a recognized breed but a type of sled dog bred specifically for performance and endurance.
