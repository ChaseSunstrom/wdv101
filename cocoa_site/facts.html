Cocoa Varieties

There are three main varieties of cocoa: Forastero, which makes up the majority of global production and offers a robust, earthy flavor; Criollo, a rare strain prized for its delicate aroma and complex taste; and Trinitario, a hybrid combining the hardiness of Forastero with the fine flavor of Criollo.
