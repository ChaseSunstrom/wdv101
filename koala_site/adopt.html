Adopt A Koala

Help support koala conservation by symbolically adopting a koala. Your adoption helps fund research, habitat protection, and rescue efforts.

Adoption Program Details

Your symbolic adoption includes a personalized certificate, a photo of your Koala, a welcome letter, and Koala stickers. You can also visit the Koala you adopted at our center.* (Admission fees apply separately.)
